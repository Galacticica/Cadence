# Sample provenance

All samples in this package are redistributable; none require payment or
permission. Provenance is recorded per source below (and mirrored in
`manifest.json`). The files in `samples/` and `tone/` are **processed**
derivatives (mono, 16-bit, trimmed, normalized, decay-capped) of the raw
recordings — see `tools/PROCESSING.md`.
